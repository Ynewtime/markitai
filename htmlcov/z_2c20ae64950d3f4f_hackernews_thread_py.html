18class HackerNewsThreadExtractor: 

19 """Extract Hacker News story threads into a ConversationThread. 

20 

21 Targets news.ycombinator.com item URLs. Parses the story text (if any) 

22 as the ``main_item`` and collects comments as ``items``, inferring 

23 parent-child relationships from the indent level of each comment row.

25 HN HTML structure targeted: 

26 - Story title: ``<span class="titleline"><a>Title</a></span>`` 

27 - Story text: ``<span class="commtext">`` in the first non-comment td 

28 - Comments: ``<tr class="athing comtr" id="...">`` 

29 - Author: ``<a class="hnuser">username</a>`` 

30 - Text: ``<span class="commtext">``

32 """
